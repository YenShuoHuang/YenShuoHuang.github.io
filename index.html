About Me

I am a PhD candidate at UCLouvain, Belgium, working on bridging urban planning and individual mobility through dynamic multi-dimensional environmental exposure assessments for sustainable soft mobility.

My research focuses on developing frameworks that integrate heat stress, air pollution, and greenery exposure data to support both urban planning decisions and citizen navigation strategies.

Research Interests

  • Urban Climate & Microclimate Modeling
  • Environmental Exposure Assessment
  • Sustainable Mobility & Active Transportation
  • Urban Planning & Health
  • Geospatial Analysis & Data Visualization
